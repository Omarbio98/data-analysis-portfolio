# Analysis of Stingless Bee Nests in Oaxaca

**Master's Thesis Project in Apicultural Production**
University of Guadalajara, 2025

## 🎯 Objective
To characterize wild stingless bee nests in the Sierra Norte of Oaxaca and demonstrate the relationship between nest structural variables.

## 📊 Methodology
- Linear, logarithmic, and power regression models to explore relationships between parameters
- Parametric and non-parametric tests to compare species
- Data processing and cleaning with tidyverse
- Visualization with ggplot2

## 🛠 Tools
R, RStudio, packages: tidyverse, ggplot2, dplyr

## 📂 Folder Contents
- `scripts/`: Documented and reproducible R code; analysis results available upon publication.

## 📄 Status
In preparation for defense (next month) and subsequent publication in an indexed journal.
